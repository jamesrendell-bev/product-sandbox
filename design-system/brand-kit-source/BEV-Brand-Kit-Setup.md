# BirdsEyeView Brand Kit — Setup & Usage

Read this once. Five minutes of setup gets you brand-correct decks every time.

---

## What's in the kit

- `BirdsEyeView-Style-Guide.pptx` — the reference deck. Doubles as your starting template.
- `BirdsEyeView-Style-Guide.md` — the written rule book. Open in any text editor.
- `BEV-Background-Title.png` — cover-slide background (wildfire imagery + ESA lockup + client logo strip locked in)
- `BEV-Background-Gradient.png` — dark content-slide background (navy → mid-blue + ESA lockup locked in)
- `BEV-Background-White.png` — light content-slide background (white + ESA lockup locked in)
- `README.txt` — short-form reference card

## ONE-TIME SETUP (≈5 minutes)

### 1. Save the kit in a fixed location

Drop the unzipped folder into a sensible spot you'll remember — `~/Documents/BEV Brand Kit/` works. Don't move the files around individually; keep them together so links from the template don't break.

### 2. Confirm Avenir Next is installed

The whole deck is set in Avenir Next, which ships with macOS. Open **Font Book** and confirm "Avenir Next" is listed (it will be). Quick visual check: open `BirdsEyeView-Style-Guide.pptx` and look at the cover title — "BirdsEyeView" should be a thin, elegant **Ultra Light** typeface. If it ever looks chunky or default-Helvetica on a new machine, the font's missing — flag it to James.

### 3. Open the style guide and read it

Open `BirdsEyeView-Style-Guide.pptx` and click through all 11 slides. Then open `BirdsEyeView-Style-Guide.md` and read the rules — particularly:

- Section 4 (Backgrounds & Lockup)
- Section 5.8 (Headline clearance)
- Section 8 (Writing & voice)

This is the only document that matters when there's a disagreement about how something should look.

## EVERY TIME YOU MAKE A NEW DECK (≈30 seconds)

### Option A — copy the style-guide deck and overwrite content (fastest)

1. Duplicate `BirdsEyeView-Style-Guide.pptx` and rename your copy (e.g. `BEV-[Client]-Proposal-[Month-Year].pptx`).
2. Delete the slides you don't need.
3. For each remaining slide, replace the placeholder text with your content. The backgrounds and lockup are already locked in — leave them alone.
4. Add new slides by duplicating an existing one (gradient or white) so the background carries over.

### Option B — start blank and apply the backgrounds yourself

1. Open PowerPoint, set slide ratio to **Widescreen 16:9 (1920 × 1080)**.
2. **Design → Format Background → Picture or Texture Fill → Insert Picture from File** → choose the appropriate PNG:
   - Slide 1 (cover) → `BEV-Background-Title.png`
   - Content slide 2 → `BEV-Background-Gradient.png`
   - Content slide 3 → `BEV-Background-White.png`
   - Slide 4 → gradient, slide 5 → white, and so on, alternating
3. Click **"Apply"** for that slide only (not "Apply to All"), since alternation matters.

## THE SIX RULES YOU CAN'T BREAK

Read these every time. They are the brand.

1. **Backgrounds alternate.** Cover = title PNG. Content slide 2 = gradient. Content slide 3 = white. And so on. Never two of the same in a row, never skip the title cover.
2. **Avenir Next, named faces only.** Use Ultra Light for display, Regular for body, Demi Bold for sub-heads and eyebrows. **Never press the Bold button.** If you want bold, change the font name to "Avenir Next Demi Bold".
3. **Hot Pink `#FF6CAA` is highlight only.** Eyebrows, rules, card titles, key numbers. Never body text. Never headlines.
4. **The pink rule sits 0.30 in below the bottom of the headline.** Look at the title slide ("Brand & Style Guide") — that's the gap. Match it everywhere. If your headline wraps to two lines, the rule drops with it.
5. **All boxes in a row are equal height. All gutters are 0.30 in. All internal padding is 0.35 in.** Never let text touch the bottom of a box.
6. **Voice = punchy, concise, never AI-shaped.** Specific numbers over vague claims. No "best in class", no "going forward", no "we are excited to". BirdsEyeView is one word. CERA® always has the ® mark.

## TROUBLESHOOTING

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| Fonts look wrong (chunky, default) | Avenir Next isn't installed. See setup step 2. |
| ESA logo wraps to two lines on slide | You've moved the background image or text-over the lockup. Re-apply the PNG via Format Background. |
| Headline runs into the BirdsEyeView/ESA lockup | Headline is too long. Shorten it. Maximum 38 characters per line. |
| Text overflows a card | Make the card taller or shorten the text. Never reduce the font size. |
| Background colour looks slightly off | Make sure you're using the PNGs from this kit, not screenshots or older versions. |
